Our Story

Blackburrow has directly impacted over 2000 women in farming across Ghana.Our cooperative unions has been able to financially help most of the women in acquiring farm inputs and stuffs they could not has achieve as individuals.

we have a market hub where where customers(wholesalers,business and retailers purchase directly from the farm or have it delivered to them at their convenience.Giving the farmer a reliable customer to produce for, and to customers, a one stop shop for farm fresh produce like tomatoes, chilies etc.
